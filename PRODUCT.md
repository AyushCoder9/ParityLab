# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Payment, platform, and reliability engineers preparing Stripe integrations for production. Evaluators and engineering stakeholders use the guided demo to understand the system quickly.

## Product Purpose

Continuously prove that payment integrations remain correct when real distributed-system failures occur, and turn failures into reproducible evidence and actionable explanations.

## Positioning

ParityLab verifies the behavior around Stripe—not merely whether an API request returned 200.

## Brand Personality

Forensic, kinetic, assured. Clear and technically exact without sounding cold.

## Anti-references

Generic neon fintech, purple-gradient SaaS, decorative glass card grids, fake terminal aesthetics, unexplained scores, and animation that delays routine work.

## Design Principles

- Show the evidence, not a claim.
- Make failure understandable.
- Spectacle teaches; product motion serves state.
- The same data powers the demo and the product.
- Safe defaults are visible defaults.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA, full keyboard operation, color-independent status, reduced-motion and reduced-data alternatives, readable technical data, and plain-language errors.
